# 📊 Online Retail Sales Dashboard

## 🚀 Project Overview

This project presents an interactive **Online Retail Sales Dashboard** built using Power BI to analyze business performance, customer behavior, and revenue trends.

The dashboard helps in understanding:

* Monthly revenue patterns 📈
* Top-performing products 🛒
* Customer distribution 🌍
* Sales contribution by country

## 📈 Insights from Dashboard

### 1. Revenue Trend Analysis

* Revenue shows steady growth from mid-year onwards
* Peak sales observed in **November and December** (holiday season impact 🎄)

### 2. Top Products Performance

* Products like **Paper Craft Items** and **Cake Stands** generate highest revenue
* Indicates strong demand for decorative and gifting items

### 3. Country-wise Sales Distribution

* 🇬🇧 **United Kingdom dominates sales (~85%)**
* Other countries like Netherlands, Germany, and France contribute smaller portions

### 4. Business Growth Pattern

* Gradual increase in revenue indicates scaling business performance
* Seasonal spikes highlight opportunities for targeted marketing
